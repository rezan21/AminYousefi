About

Mohammad Amin Yousefi is an Artist from Esfahan, Iran. He’s Studying his Bachelor’s degree in Art in Photography at the Art University of Isfahan. His work deals with themes related to the cityscape, environment, humanity, effects of war, and how the act of photography can conceptually mirror the structures of these relationships. He has established his career and artistic personality through several exhibitions, teaching, and photography courses. In recent years, he has further followed his performance in the field of photography by participating in group exhibitions and photo expos.
